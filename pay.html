General Statistics ("User Interface Designer Salary")

A User Interface Designer earns an average salary of $49,886 per year. A skill in Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), which is a markup language, is associated with high pay. Experience has a moderate effect on income for this job and people in this job generally do not have more than 20 years' experience.
