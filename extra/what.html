What is Airbnb?

Airbnb is a website dominating the industry of travel. It allows people to list, find, and rent lodging. It has over 1,500,000 listings in 34,000 cities and 190 countries.

Founded in August 2008 and headquartered in San Francisco, California, the company is privately owned and operated by Airbnb, Inc. The company is valued at $20 billion.

Airbnb began when two designers had space to share and hosted three travellers looking for a place to stay. Now, millions of hosts and travellers use Airbnb to list their space to users and also book their own unique accommodations anywhere in the world.
